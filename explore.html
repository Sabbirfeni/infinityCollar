How do Infinity Collars work?

You create a virtual fence in our app by defining the boundaries on the map. These boundaries can be designed for a specific location, or they can be generic boundaries to be used on the go.

Infinity Collar then tracks your pup’s movement and predicts their path, and responds as they near the boundaries you drew

The collar escalates its response based on the the behavior of your pet. You choose how your dog is corrected and the level of intensity. The typical pattern, the collar will first beep to alert your dog of a nearing boundary. If your dog nears a boundary, the beeping will gradually intensify to encourage your dog to change their path. If your dog is determined to keep going the collar vibrates. Lastly, if your dog attempts to enter the restricted area the collar uses a mild form of electronic stimulation to get their attention.

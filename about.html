How we got started

In 2017, there was a dog named Tex. She was riding in a jeep and enjoying the mountains of Tennessee with her owner, Dial. Then, she spotted a deer off the trail and left out after it. In his picnic, Dial knew he needed to find a better way if he wanted to keep sharing about how to keep her safe on these wildness adventures with Tex. He worried about how to keep her safe on these journeys and still give her the freedom she deserves. Short of a leash, there wasn't a truly mobile solution that wasn't wholly dependent on cellular service Tex could enjoy.

So Dial partnered with Silas. They asked themselves, "What if all of your dog's containment systems could be controlled with a phone or a handheld remote instead of bulky or expensive solutions?" Together they designed a revolutionary collar that can truly be used anywhere. They were adamant that their collar be a complete solution for dog owners. Infinity Collar replaces not only fences but also leashes and baby gates.

Why we do & what we do

How much more enjoyable is it to travel with your dog if the only things you need to remember fit in your pocket? They believed this gives you the freedom to enjoy more adventures together.
Dial and Silas set out with a simple singular mission: To simplify dog ownership.
